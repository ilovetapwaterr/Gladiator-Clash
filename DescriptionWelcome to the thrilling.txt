Description:Welcome to the thrilling world of the Gladiator Clash! Step into the shoes of a fearless gladiator who must fight hordes of relentless skeletons to survive. This action-packed game will test your skills, reflexes, and strategic thinking as you battle your way through the arena. The game only has 1 game mode. In the Survival mode, you must fight off endless waves of skeletons, each wave more difficult than the last. Your goal is to see how long you can survive and to pass as many waves as possible. You can use medkits to help you heal when fighting the skeletons because they do alot of damage.
